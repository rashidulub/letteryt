Dear Rimu

 I love you. Just like the shy glance of a newlywed bride on the wedding night, the comforting warmth of a blanket on a chilly winter night, a reliable umbrella in a drizzling rain, the soothing southern breeze, or the moonlit glow in the night sky. I love you. Just like the clouds love their rain, the night loves the moon, and me? I love only you.
I love you. Loving means loving. This love makes my heart restless, this love never lets me stay at ease, this love cannot be easily restrained. As I love, I drown in you. I love you. Yes, I love only you. I love you so much that I forget to love myself. I cannot stop loving you, just like a river that flows without a shore, just like how your deep, kohl-lined eyes shake me from within.
Just like that, I love only you, my love.
